B.C.A (Bachelor of Computer Application)

B.C.A is acronym of Bachelor of Computer Application. This degree course is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.

Scope

After completion of B.C.A. the students can join any software industry or can seek admission for computer P.G. Courses like M. C. S., M.Sc. (Comp. Sci. & Information Technology),M.C.A., M.C.M. and M.B.A..

Eligibility

The candidates who have Passed 10+2 and scored 45% 12th HSC Examination in Any Stream are eligible for this course. Admission to this three- year degree course is strictly on merit basis, based on objective type questions followed by group discussion and personal interviews.

Registration

Prospective candidates may register on payment of the following.

Prospectus ₹50

Documents

In order to take admission, respective documents are as follow:

  • 10th Marksheet (only Xerox 2 copies)
  • 12th Marksheet (Original + 3 xerox copies)
  • Leaving Certificate of last school/college (3 copies)
  • Migration Certificate (For students coming from other States) (3 copies)
  • Caste Certificate

Admission

The candidates with 45% or above marks will be registered and admitted to this course as per their merit position. The intake capacity of the course may vary. Every students have to fulfill admission procedure by paying requisite fees and submitting the original documents before due date or else the next candidate in the waiting list will be admitted.

Fee Structure

The fees structure may vary.

Programme Outcomes (POs)

  • PO-1: Apply knowledge of computing fundamentals, mathematics and domain knowledge appropriate for the conceptualization of computing models.
  • PO-2: Identify, analyze, formulate, design and develop the real world requirements by critical thinking for complex problems in IT enabled services.
  • PO-3: Recognize the need and adopt appropriate tools and techniques for modern computing practices.
  • PO-4: Make use of ethical practices and cyber regulations in the computing field for managing software projects.
  • PO-5: Understand the societal, environmental and moral values and its impact with respect to computing, communication, literary and professional practice

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  • PSO-1: To produce skill oriented human resource.
  • PSO-2: To import practical skills among students.
  • PSO-3: To make industry ready resource.
  • PSO-4: Students get the knowledge of relational databases.
  • PSO-5: To know the training and development methods of employees of employee’s skills in organization.
