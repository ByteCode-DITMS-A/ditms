Courses We Offer

This degree course is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.

B.C.S
(B.Sc.Computer Science)

After completion of B.C.S. the students can join any software industry or can seek admission for computer P.G. Courses like M. C. S., M.Sc. (Comp. Sci. & Information Technology),M.C.A., M.C.M. and M.B.A.

B.C.A.
(Bachelor of Computer Application)

After completion of B.C.A. the students can join industry or can enroll to P.G. Courses like M.C.A., M.C.M. and M.B.A.

B.B.A
(Bachelor of Business Administration)

After completion of B.B.A. the students can enroll to M.B.A. in any University.
