B.B.A(Bachelor of Business Administration)

B.B.A is acronym of Bachelor of Business Administration. This degree course is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.

Scope

After completion of B.B.A, the students can unroll to M.B.A in any University

Eligibility

The candidates who have Passed 10+2 /12th/ HSC Examination in Any Stream are eligible for this course. Admission to this three- year degree course is strictly on merit basis, based on objective type questions followed by group discussion and personal interviews.

Registration

Prospective candidates may register on payment of the following.

Prospectus ₹50

Documents

In order to take admission, respective documents are as follow:

  • 10th Marksheet (only Xerox 2 copies)
  • 12th Marksheet (Original + 3 xerox copies)
  • Leaving Certificate of last school/college (3 copies)
  • Migration Certificate (For students coming from other States) (3 copies)
  • Caste Certificate

Admission

The candidates with 45% or above marks will be registered and admitted to this course as per their merit position. The intake capacity of the course may vary. Every students have to fulfill admission procedure by paying requisite fees and submitting the original documents before due date or else the next candidate in the waiting list will be admitted.

Fee Structure

The fees structure may vary.

Programme Outcomes (POs)

  • PO-1: To provide adequate basic understanding about management education among the students.
  • PO-2: To prepare students to explore opportunities being newly created in the management profession.
  • PO-3: To train the students in communication skills effectively.
  • PO-4: To develop appropriate skills amongst students so as to make them competent for self-employment.
  • PO-5: To inculcate Entrepreneurial skills.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  • PSO-1: Students will exhibit understanding of broad business concepts and principles.
  • PSO-2: Students will exhibit critical thinking skills to address diverse business challenges and opportunities.
  • PSO-3: Students will be able to identify and define problems and opportunities.
  • PSO-4: Students will demonstrate problem solving skills by gathering and assessing appropriate information.
  • PSO-5: Students will demonstrate use of appropriate techniques to effectively manage business challenges.
